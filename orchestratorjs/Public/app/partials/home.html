Open Source Platform for creating multi-device applications

Orchestrator.js is an open source platform for creating applications to heterogeneous and multi-device environment.
Make your devices to communicate seamlessly, and put their resouces into use!


Orchestrator.js Features

Building apps is easy

The aim of Social Devices is to increase, facilitate, and enrich social interactions between people in various kinds of co-located and face-to-face situations. For example, when people meet, the devices can greet each other aloud to help people to remember each others’ names. Also device-to-device interaction can be enriched: Social Devices can make otherwise invisible device interaction explicit to users.

Context is the key

A precondition of Social Devices is the availability of computationally capable smart devices that are aware of and can interact with other devices and users in their proximity. The apps are started in a proactive manner without user initiation. Additionally, a user can start an app which then suggest to other users in the vicinity to take part to some interaction.

Cloud-based system

The Social Devices Platform currently runs in Amazon cloud, consisting a number of cloud services. The infrastructure abstracts the differences of the devices. However, instead of hiding or ignoring the different resources of each device, Social Devices accents the differences by regarding them as capabilities, which describe what a device can do. The client side currently consists of Android devices and Python capable devices, such as Linux laptops and MeeGo phones.
